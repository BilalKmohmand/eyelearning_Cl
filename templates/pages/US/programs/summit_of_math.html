LEARNING PROCESS

Students are assessed and provided with personalized questions on a daily basis, getting a step closer to math mastery everyday.

  • 1 Preparation

    Students check the lesson objectives and sample questions to speculate on their understanding of the concepts.

  • 2 Problem
    Solving

    Student follows along the questions and utilizes walk-me through functions for struggling points.

  • 3 Issue
    Finding

    Summit of Math will drill-down to the core and find out why the student is struggling.

  • 4 Targeted
    Questions

    Student will be given new targeted questions based on the analysis made.

  • 5 Feedback &
    Consultation

    Instructor will monitor student's work and provide feedback. Summit of Math will accumulate student data and generate progress reports.
