WHY EYE LEVEL

Who We AreWe are a leading supplemental education service provider focused on developing
our students to be critical thinkers and lifelong learners.

  • 1:1 Coaching

    Providing an individualized program to each student has always been a core value of an Eye Level education.

  • 2 Million
    Enrollment
    Worldwide

    Throughout the years, Eye Level has nurtured millions of students worldwide.

  • 20 Countries

    Eye Level is now available in 20 countries and regions around the world and the number continues to grow.

  • 40 + Years

    Daekyo, the parent company of Eye Level, has long been recognized as one of the leading educational and cultural service providers.

Eye Level education starts with understanding a child's individual
learning needs, enabling them to progress at their own pace.

Study ProcessAt Eye Level, we provide systematic individualized learning.

  1. 1 Diagnostic Test

    Prior to each session, every child is individually assessed.

  2. 2 1:1 Coaching

    Instructors customize the curriculum for students and provide guidance throughout the program.

  3. 3 Self-Directed Learning

    Students develop independent learning skills at our Eye Level learning centers.

  4. 4 Mastery Learning

    We determine the mastery of each lesson based on study accuracy and study time consumption.

Learning CenterComing in to the center removes distraction factors and helps students build self-directed learning habits.

Daily Class Flow
1. Check in

Students check in when they arrive at the learning center.

2. Prepare for class

Students take out their own folder from the shelf.

3. Receive 1 on 1 Coaching

Review last week's progress.

Be guided this week's learning concept and coverage.

4. Self-directed learning

Work on today's assignment.

Solve booklets and listen to audio.

Make corrections based on instructor's feedback.

5. Receive feedback and wrap-up

Check out and wrap up.

Work on supplemental materials.

6. Check out

Students check out when they leave the learning center.

With Eye Level, Your Child Can
  • Be confident in solving problems
  • Develop a habit of independent learning
  • Get a genuine interest in Math and English
